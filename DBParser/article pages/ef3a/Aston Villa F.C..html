{{Infobox Football club |clubname=Aston Villa F.C. |current= |fullname=Aston Villa Football Club |nickname= |founded=1874 |ground=Villa Park |capacity=42,640 |chairman=Tony Xia |manager=Steve Bruce |captain= |league=Championship |season=2015/16 |position=Premier League, 20th }} Aston Villa Football Club, is an English football club.

It is owned by Tony Xia, a Chinese businessman who also owns a company called Recon Group.

They were started in 1874. They play at Villa Park in Birmingham. Since the inauguration of the Premier League they were a participant until relegation in 2016.
