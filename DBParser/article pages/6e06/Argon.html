|isotopes comment= |discovered by=Lord Rayleigh and William Ramsay |discovery date=1894 |first isolation by=Lord Rayleigh and William Ramsay |first isolation date=1894 }} Argon is a chemical element. The symbol for argon is Ar, and its atomic number (or proton number) is 18. It is a noble gas and no electrons or protons can be lost or gained from this atom.

Argon atoms are found in air. About 1% of the Earth's atmosphere (the air around us) is argon.
