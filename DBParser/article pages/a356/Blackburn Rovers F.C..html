Blackburn Rovers F.C.[1] is an English football club. They play at Ewood Park, which is in Blackburn, Lancashire.[2] The manager of the club is Gary Bowyer.[3]

History

Blackburn Rovers won the FA Premier League once in the 1994/1995 season.[4] However, in 1999, they were relegated.
