{{main|Track and field athletics}}

The long jump is a sport in athletics (track and field), where athletes compete by comparing lengths of their jumps where athletes try to jump as far as they can. For this, the athlete runs down the runway to the jumping pit which is made of soft sand[1] If a person falls backwards when jumping, this can decrease the length of their jump.
