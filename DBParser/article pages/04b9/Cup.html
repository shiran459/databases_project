{{wiktionary|cup}}

A cup is any kind of container used for holding liquid and drinking. These include:

  • teacup
  • paper cup

Cup may also mean:

  • Measuring cup, a measuring instrument for liquids and powders, used primarily in cooking
  • Cup (unit), a customary unit of volume and measure
  • Cancer of unknown primary origin, form of cancer
  • The cup of a brassière, the part that covers the breasts
  • A cup-shaped trophy or award for winning in a sport
